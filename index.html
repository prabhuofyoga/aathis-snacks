Our Story

Welcome to Aathi's Snaacks. We believe food is a memory. It’s the smell of hot oil in a Thoothukudi bakery and the earthy aroma of palm jaggery in Kovilpatti.

In a world of mass-produced snacks, we went back to our roots. We don't just 'make' snacks; we curate heritage. Every packet you open is a tribute to the culinary masters of our hometowns.
